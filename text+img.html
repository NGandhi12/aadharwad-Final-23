NGO for Physically Challenged in Mumbai

At Adharwad, we understand that serious illness can be a challenging journey. Our dedicated bedridden and critical care unit is designed to provide compassionate and comprehensive care to those in need. Our team of skilled healthcare professionals is committed to ensuring the comfort, dignity, and well-being of our patients. Adharwad also offers educational resources, vocational training, and community programs to help individuals overcome physical limitations and lead empowered lives. We aim to create an inclusive society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Join us to make a lasting difference.
